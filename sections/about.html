About me

Cira Valenzuela started performing since she was 5yrs old as a dancer. She fell in love with acting when she joined theater at Clark High School at the age of 16yrs. Since she has attended coaching seminars with Casting Directors Lisa London, Michael Tesla, Helen McCready, Susan Putnam, Andy Hendy, etc. Cira has also done stunt training mainly on high falls and choreographed combat training.

Ms. Valenzuela has appeared in films such as Attack of the Unknown, Aidy Kaine, Last Round, Station to Station, and Harlee’s Roses. Cira has been apart of several commercials and ad campaigns.
